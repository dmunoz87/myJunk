New York City is the most populous and most densely populated city in the United States. Known as the "Melting Pot", it is home to a wide variety of races and nationalities. It's a hub for entertainment, with 4 of America's major broadcast networks headquartered there as well as the iconic New Year's Eve ceremony where millions go to watch the ball drop in Times Square every year. New York City is a one-stop-shop when it comes to experiencing everything that there is in life.

Thank you for visiting the homepage for everything that is New York City. We are devoted to bringing you the lastest info about events, weather, and the environment of New York City. Feel free to hang out, surf, and learn a little something new about The City.
